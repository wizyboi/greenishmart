Our Story: Redefining Online Shopping

Discover how GreenishMart grew from a small startup to your favorite online shopping destination, committed to sustainability and customer satisfaction.

Our Journey

Founded in 2018 by three college friends who shared a passion for sustainable living and technology, GreenishMart began as a small online marketplace for eco-friendly products. What started as a weekend project in a garage quickly grew into something much bigger.

We noticed that while e-commerce was booming, very few platforms focused on sustainability and ethical sourcing. Our mission was clear: create an online shopping experience that not only offered great products but also made a positive impact on the planet.

Today, GreenishMart serves millions of customers across the globe, offering everything from everyday essentials to specialty eco-products. But our core values remain the same: sustainability, transparency, and exceptional customer experience.

Key Milestones

  • 2018: Launched with just 50 eco-friendly products
  • 2019: Reached 100,000 customers and expanded to 500+ products
  • 2020: Introduced carbon-neutral shipping nationwide
  • 2021: Expanded internationally to 20+ countries
  • 2022: Launched GreenishMart Foundation for environmental initiatives
  • 2023: Awarded "Most Sustainable E-commerce Platform"

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make sustainable shopping accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone. We're committed to providing high-quality eco-friendly products while maintaining transparent business practices and minimizing our environmental footprint.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading sustainable e-commerce platform, inspiring positive change in consumer behavior and setting new standards for environmental responsibility in online retail.

Our Promise

Every purchase you make contributes to environmental initiatives. We donate 1% of all profits to reforestation projects and partner with organizations working to combat climate change.

Our Core Values

Sustainability First

Every decision we make is evaluated through an environmental lens. From packaging to shipping, we prioritize eco-friendly solutions.

Transparency

We believe in honest business practices. You can trace the origin of products and see exactly how your purchase makes a difference.

Community Focus

We're building more than a marketplace - we're creating a community of conscious consumers who share our values.

Innovation

We constantly explore new technologies and methods to reduce our environmental impact and improve your shopping experience.

Ethical Partnerships

We carefully vet all our suppliers and brands to ensure they meet our strict ethical and environmental standards.

Customer Delight

Your satisfaction is our priority. We go above and beyond to ensure every shopping experience is exceptional.
