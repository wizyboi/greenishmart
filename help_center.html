Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

+

You can track your order in several ways:

  • Visit the "Track Order" page and enter your order number
  • Check your email for tracking updates
  • Use our mobile app to view real-time delivery status
  • Contact customer support with your order number

Tracking information is typically available 24 hours after your order is shipped.

How do I cancel or modify an order?

+

You can cancel or modify your order within 1 hour of placing it:

  • Go to "My Orders" in your account
  • Find the order you want to change
  • Click "Cancel Order" or "Modify Order"
  • Follow the prompts to complete the process

After 1 hour, orders enter the processing stage and cannot be modified. You'll need to wait for delivery and then initiate a return if needed.

What are your shipping options and costs?

+

We offer several shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 business days): Free on orders over $50, otherwise $4.99
  • Express Shipping (2-3 business days): $9.99
  • Next-Day Delivery: $14.99 (order before 2 PM local time)
  • Same-Day Delivery: $19.99 (available in select cities)

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and order size.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 10-21 business days depending on the destination.

Please note:

  • International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes
  • Shipping costs vary by country and order weight
  • Some product restrictions may apply based on destination country

You can see available countries and shipping costs during checkout.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items:

  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Return shipping is free for defective or incorrect items
  • Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days
  • Some items (electronics, perishables) may have different return windows

To start a return:

  1. Go to "My Orders" in your account
  2. Select the item(s) you want to return
  3. Choose a return reason and method
  4. Print the return label and ship the package

How do I reset my password?

+

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for the password reset link
  5. Click the link and create a new password

If you don't receive the email within 15 minutes:

  • Check your spam/junk folder
  • Make sure you're using the correct email address
  • Contact customer support for assistance

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • GreenishMart Gift Cards

All payments are processed securely using 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your full credit card information on our servers.

Why was my payment declined?

+

Common reasons for payment declines include:

  • Insufficient funds in your account
  • Incorrect card details (number, expiration date, CVV)
  • Card has expired
  • International transaction blocked by your bank
  • Daily transaction limit reached

What to try:

  1. Verify your payment information is correct
  2. Contact your bank to ensure the transaction is authorized
  3. Try a different payment method
  4. Wait a few minutes and try again
